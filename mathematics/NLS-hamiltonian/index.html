Let

d⁢H⁢(u)⁢v = dd⁢ϵ|ϵ=0⁢H⁢(u+ϵ⁢v)

Therefore we have for v∈Tu that

-2⁢ℑ⁢∫XH⁢(u)⁢v¯⁢𝑑x = ℜ⁢∫(-Δ⁢u+2⁢μ⁢|u|p-1⁢u)⁢v¯⁢𝑑x

It follows that

-2⁢XH⁢(u)=-i⁢Δ⁢u+2⁢i⁢μ⁢|u|p-1⁢u,

i.e.,

XH⁢(u)=i2⁢Δ⁢u-i⁢μ⁢|u|p-1⁢u.
